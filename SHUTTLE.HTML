Volvo MODEL - X

Volvo Buses exemplify a commitment to passenger safety and comfort. Equipped with Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) including collision warning, emergency braking, and lane-keeping support, these buses provide a secure travel experience. Reinforced structures and advanced fire safety systems further enhance passenger protection. Anti-slip flooring, sturdy handrails, and easy-access doors ensure safe boarding and exiting. Visibility is maximized through high-quality lighting systems and large windows. Seat capacity varies by model: city buses typically seat 30-50 passengers with standing room, intercity buses accommodate 45-60 seated passengers, and premium coaches can seat up to 80, featuring reclining seats and onboard entertainment for long-distance journeys. Volvo Buses' dedication to innovation ensures both safety and comfort, making them a top choice for modern transportation solutions.
